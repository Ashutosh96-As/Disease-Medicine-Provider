Daily Health Tips

  • Stay Hydrated

    Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily to maintain proper hydration and support overall health.

  • Get Adequate Sleep

    Aim for 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night to support immune function and mental clarity.

  • Practice Mindful Eating

    Pay attention to your food, eat slowly, and listen to your body's hunger and fullness cues.

  • Take Regular Breaks

    If you sit for long periods, take short breaks every hour to stretch and move around.

  • Maintain Social Connections

    Regular social interaction can improve mental health and reduce the risk of depression.
